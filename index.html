The problem

Your office runs on cash. Your records still run on hope.

When collections live in one ledger, deposits in another, and driver balances in someone's head, the office doesn't have control. It has habits. And habits break the moment a route comes up short, a deposit slips a day, or the principal asks for a number nobody can produce on the spot.

  • End-of-day reconciliation that steals hours from people already exhausted
  • Deposits and collections that never quite add up. And nobody can prove why
  • Driver accountability that depends on memory instead of a permanent record

The solution

One ledger. One truth. From the first collection to the last deposit.

Distribution Life gives your office a single system of record. Every collection tied to its route and driver, every deposit matched to what came in, every expense logged against treasury. Close the day with confidence, not crossed fingers. When something doesn't balance, you'll know exactly what, who, and when.

  • Same-day collections with full route and driver traceability
  • Deposits reconciled against collections. Gaps surface immediately
  • Print-ready reports your accountant, auditor, and principal will respect

Capabilities

Every module earns its place. Because operators asked for it

No bloat. No features nobody uses. Four core workflows that hold a distributor office together. Each one shaped by people who've done the job.

Route Collections

Capture every rupee the moment a route returns. Cash, checks, and credit. Against the driver and manifest. Know instantly which routes are settled and which are still open.

Bank Deposits

Log deposits by account and tie them to the day's intake. Undeposited cash never hides in a drawer. It shows up on screen before it becomes a crisis.

Treasury & Expenses

Fuel, allowances, petty cash, operating costs. All tracked against a live treasury balance with a clear approval trail. Your cash position, always current.

Driver Accountability & Reports

Full per-driver shortage and overage history, plus daily, weekly, and monthly reports formatted for your printer. Ready for signatures, filing, and audit.

How it works

The same three steps your best office manager already follows

Distribution Life doesn't reinvent your day. It organises it. From route return to locked door, in the order you already work.

  1. 1

    Capture what came in

    Trucks return. Receipts get encoded. Cash, coins, checks, bank deposits. Each tied to the driver and route it belongs to. No loose papers. No "we'll do it tomorrow."

  2. 2

    Match deposits and expenses

    Record what went to the bank and what left the treasury. The system reconciles against collections in real time. If the numbers don't agree, you'll see the gap before you leave the building.

  3. 3

    Print, sign, and close

    Generate the daily summary, driver accountability sheet, and treasury report. Signed, filed, and done. The way a professional office finishes every day.
